# Community & Feedback Guidelines

DartDL is a professional project dedicated to providing the best downloading experience for Android. While this is no longer an open-source project, we value our community and welcome constructive feedback.

## Support Contact

If you encounter issues or have suggestions for improvements, please reach out to us:
**support@amanblaze.in**

## Guidelines for Engagement

1. **Constructive Feedback**: When reporting issues, please provide as much detail as possible (device model, Android version, and URL being downloaded).
2. **Respectful Communication**: We expect all interactions with the support team to be polite and respectful.
3. **Responsible Use**: Please use this application in compliance with the laws of your jurisdiction and the terms of service of the websites you interact with.

## Scope

These guidelines apply only to direct communications with the author (Amanblaze) regarding the DartDL application.
