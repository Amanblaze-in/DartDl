# Community & Feedback Guidelines

DartDL is a project dedicated to providing the best downloading experience for Android. We value our community and welcome constructive feedback.

## Support & Feedback

If you encounter issues or have suggestions for improvements, please reach out to the project maintainers or submit an issue on the repository.

## Guidelines for Engagement

1. **Constructive Feedback**: When reporting issues, please provide as much detail as possible (device model, Android version, and URL being downloaded).
2. **Respectful Communication**: We expect all interactions to be polite and respectful.
3. **Responsible Use**: Please use this application in compliance with the laws of your jurisdiction and the terms of service of the websites you interact with.

## Scope

These guidelines apply to the DartDL application and community.
